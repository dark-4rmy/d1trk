About D1TRK

Welcome to D1TRK Tunes! Here, we dive into the world of electronic and hardstyle music, driven by the beats and rhythms that inspire us to dance and connect with fans around the world. D1TRK, a Moroccan artist influenced by the energy and intensity of uptempo beats, takes listeners on a journey through high-energy tracks. Known for remixing iconic songs like *Señorita* and *Oh My* with a hardstyle twist, D1TRK blends old-school vibes with modern kicks for a unique sound.

Music Style and Vision

The D1TRK experience brings together genres like hardstyle and reverse bass to create an electrifying atmosphere meant for dancing and immersion. Inspired by artists such as Dimitri K and Dr. Peacock, D1TRK is dedicated to making each track a memorable sonic journey, celebrating the dynamic, powerful rhythms of hardstyle.

What's in Store?
